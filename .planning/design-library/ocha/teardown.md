# OCHA — brand/design teardown (founder's leading direction)

**Source:** https://ocha.framer.website/ — "OCHA — Brutalist Matcha Bar in Tampa."
A Framer **template** ("Remix this template · Designed by Ambie"), analyzed from
its live HTML/CSS on 2026-07-15.
**Why it's here:** the founder shared it as *"the perfect illustration of how I
think the brand should look"* for Toofies. **Status: 🟡 REFERENCE / leading
design direction — NOT a ratified decision.** Design (D11–D15) is still open; this
is grounding for the design gate, to be turned into options + a spec, then
ratified. Nothing here is applied to the app yet.

---

## 1. Brand essence (what it's really selling)

OCHA does **not** sell a drink — it sells a **ritual + lifestyle + community**.
"A ritual for people romanticizing everyday life, one matcha at a time." The
product is the *feeling* (calm energy, belonging, everyday romance); the matcha
is the vehicle. **This is the single most important transferable idea for
Toofies:** sell the *ritual of mindful, joyful dessert* and the community around
it — not "tracking." It's the guilt-free, enjoy-your-life positioning, expressed
as a brand.

## 2. Palette (real hex, by prominence)

| Role | Hex | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Ground / paper** | `#fefae7` | warm cream/ivory — the dominant background |
| **Ink** | `#000` (+ `#121212`, `#141414`) | true black — brutalist contrast |
| **Primary brand** | `#03563e` (deep matcha) | + `#047052`, `#23783b` mid-greens |
| **Accent — soft** | `#ffbad8` | blossom / "vintage pink" — the playful pop |
| **Accent — punch** | `#c6ff3a` | acid lime — high-energy highlight (sparingly) |
| Supporting neutrals | `#f4f1ea`, `#bdbdbd`, `#8a8a82` | off-white + greys |

**System:** warm cream + black ink + deep green, with **blossom pink** and **acid
lime** as the joy/energy pops. Warm, natural, appetizing, premium-but-playful.

## 3. Typography

- **Display:** **Anton SC** — heavy, condensed, **UPPERCASE** poster grotesque.
  The "brutalist shout": OCHA, section labels (SPECIALS, MERCH, COMMUNITY),
  big statements. Oversized, tight, confident.
- **Body / UI:** **Inter** — clean neutral sans for menu items, descriptions,
  nav.
- Uppercase labels with modest tracking (`letter-spacing: 0.06em`).
- **Pairing = loud condensed display + quiet neutral body.** One face carries all
  the personality; the other stays out of the way.

## 4. Shape & layout — "soft brutalism," not harsh

- Despite the "brutalist" label, containers are **rounded**: `border-radius: 16px`
  (35×) and `24px` (18×), plus pills (`100px`). So it's **approachable
  brutalism** — brutalist *type* + warm palette + **soft rounded cards/buttons**.
  Important: it is bold, not cold.
- **Editorial devices:** triple-repeated word labels (OCHA OCHA OCHA · SPECIALS
  SPECIALS SPECIALS), a live clock + "SCROLL" cue in the hero, big priced menu
  rows with witty one-liners, product/merch cards, an events/workshops grid, map,
  blog, FAQ.
- Grid-forward, generous, poster-like sections.

## 5. Motion

- **Marquees / tickers** are the signature: scrolling "THE BEST PLANS START WITH A
  MATCHA ·" and repeating section words. Kinetic, lively, but simple.
- Otherwise restraint — the energy is in type + marquee, not heavy effects.

## 6. Voice & tone — ⭐ the most transferable asset

Playful, witty, self-aware, warm, lifestyle-first. Product copy has *personality*,
never specs:
- "The kind of order you'll accidentally make your whole personality for a while."
- "basically the beverage equivalent of your favorite oversized hoodie on rainy
  afternoons."
- "Your new personality trait, but make it wearable."
- "liquid vibes and stone-ground fuel for the creative class."
- FAQ: "Can I come alone?" · "Will I stay longer than planned?"

This voice is **almost exactly Toofies' target tone** (playful, guilt-free,
enjoy-your-life). It's the clearest thing to borrow.

## 7. What Toofies BORROWS / ADAPTS / REJECTS (the honest mapping)

**Borrow (strong fit):**
- The **lifestyle/ritual/community brand strategy** (sell the joy + belonging, not
  the tracker).
- The **voice** — witty, warm, personality-rich microcopy.
- **Warm cream ground + black ink + one loud condensed display face** paired with a
  quiet body sans.
- **Blossom pink** as a playful accent (also validated by Crumbl's mono+pink in the
  library) and the **soft-brutalist rounded cards**.
- **Marquee/kinetic type** for celebration moments (streaks, milestones).

**Adapt (needs a Toofies-specific move):**
- **Matcha green as *primary* reads "tea/health/wellness,"** which can tension with
  **dessert** (dessert cues warm — pink, cream, caramel/cocoa). Options: keep a
  green as a *secondary* "fresh/health" note but let a **dessert-warm hue** (berry
  pink? caramel? cocoa?) lead — or deliberately invert to green-forward if the
  founder wants the "healthy indulgence" read. **Open — for the gate.**
- **Brutalism must stay *soft*.** The brand is guilt-free and non-punitive
  (locked safeguards); harsh/cold brutalism would fight that. Keep the bold type,
  keep the rounded warmth.
- **Acid lime** is high-energy — use only as a tiny accent, if at all.

**Reject / caution:**
- It's a **purchasable template** (many will use it) — Toofies needs its own spin
  to not look generic. Take the *system*, not the layout wholesale.
- Don't import "abstinence/discipline" energy — OCHA's is pure enjoyment, which is
  correct for us; keep it that way (safeguard #1).

### Candidate direction this suggests (un-ratified)
"**Soft-brutalist dessert ritual**": cream paper (`#fefae7`), black ink, a bold
condensed uppercase display face, a warm dessert-led accent (berry/blossom pink)
with an optional fresh-green secondary, rounded cards, kinetic marquee for
celebrations, and witty guilt-free voice. The 🦷 mascot + SPEAKER-style card
patterns from the library slot into this.
