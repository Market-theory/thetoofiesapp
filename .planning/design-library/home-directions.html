What to do with these

Tell me which direction feels most like Toofies — or take the streak card from one, the calm of another, the colors of a third. There’s no wrong answer here; the point is to react. Whatever you land on gets written into the design spec and the decision register, and only then does it become the real look.

Every direction already honors the locked rules: guilt-free voice, no calories or weight, a forgiving streak, and a treat that’s celebrated rather than policed.
